About Patched

On a mission to help do-it-yourselfers own their home projects, Patched is a platform that connects users with service professionals when and where they’re needed most. With certified plumbers, electricians and general professionals, Patched is a the destination for tapping into the decades of expertise, advice, and experience that our pros have. Using intuitive online tools, Patched allows users to schedule video chats so that our Patched Pros can visually diagnose the problem and provide a solution. We offer convenient availability to our fully insured Patched Pro team, and our 100% Money Back Guarantees ensures that we’ll always strive for our users to be satisfied with every experience. Own your DIY project with Patched by your side.

Our Story

Over 70% of homeowners tackle DIY projects to save money around the house but many feel their way through the dark by watching television shows, scouring the Internet, or simply asking a friend. Patched was founded in 2016 on the simple principle that there’s got to be a better way of doing this. We found that DIYers like us seek professional advice in a convenient on-demand setting, but that nothing was really meeting our needs. At the same time, many home repair experts nearing retirement were looking for more flexible schedules and opportunities to earn additional income and we recognized that we’d be able to help connect the two sides of the home repair equation. Since then, we’ve been putting our idea into practice with friends and family, enthusiastic DIYers, and home repair experts to make sure that the service seamlessly integrates with your home repair projects and we’re excited for you to give us a try.
